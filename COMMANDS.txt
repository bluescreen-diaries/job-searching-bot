JOB BOT - COMMANDS & FLAGS
==========================


ADDING COMPANIES
----------------

/add <company name>
  Auto-discovers the company's career page URL.
  Example: /add Google

/add <company name> | <category>
  Auto-discovers the URL and filters jobs by department.
  Example: /add Google | IT
  Example: /add Amazon | Warehouse, Logistics

/addurl <name> <url>
  Adds a company with a direct URL.
  Example: /addurl "Home Depot" https://careers.homedepot.com

/addurl <name> <url> <category>
  Adds a company with a direct URL and department filter.
  Example: /addurl "Home Depot" https://careers.homedepot.com IT

/addurl <name> <url> browser
  Forces the bot to use a real browser for this site.
  Use this for sites with search forms or JS-rendered content.
  Example: /addurl "Chase" https://jpmc.fa.oraclecloud.com/... browser


BULK IMPORT
-----------

/bulkadd
  Attach a .txt file to this command to add multiple companies at once.
  One company per line.

  Supported formats in the txt file:
    Company Name
    Company Name | Category
    Company Name | Category1, Category2
    Company Name https://direct-url.com
    Company Name https://direct-url.com | Category

  Example file:
    Google
    Stripe | Engineering, Data
    Home Depot https://careers.homedepot.com | Warehouse
    Chase https://jpmc.fa.oraclecloud.com/... browser


REMOVING COMPANIES
------------------

/remove <company name>
  Stops watching a company.
  Example: /remove Google


PREFERENCES
-----------

/setpref keywords <keywords>
  Sets job title keywords. Comma separated. Applies to all companies.
  Example: /setpref keywords IT support, help desk, technician

/setpref location <location>
  Sets your preferred location (informational, used in searches).
  Example: /setpref location remote

/prefs
  Shows all current preferences.


VIEWING & SEARCHING
-------------------

/list
  Shows all tracked companies with their ATS type and category filter.

/search
  Runs a job search right now instead of waiting for 8 AM.

/help2
  Shows the command list inside Discord.


NOTES
-----
- The bot runs a search automatically every day at 8 AM.
- It only posts jobs it hasn't seen before (no duplicates).
- Keywords filter applies globally to all companies.
- Category filter applies per company and checks job title, department, and description.
- A job must match BOTH keywords AND category (if set) to be posted.
- Sites that require login or CAPTCHA cannot be scraped.
- For sites with search forms or dropdowns, use the "browser" flag with /addurl.
